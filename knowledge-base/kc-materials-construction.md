# Kitchen Crest Cabinets — Materials & Construction

## Door Construction
- **Frame**: Solid wood (NOT particle board)
- **Center Panel**: Fiberboard — engineered for stability, NOT particle board
- **Style Options**: Shaker (5-piece) or Recessed Panel (Estate)
- **Finish**: Factory-applied, color-matched to line specification

## Cabinet Box Construction
- **Material**: Furniture-grade plywood and engineered wood
- **Interior Finish**: UV-coated natural wood
- **Shelving**: Adjustable, included with every cabinet
- **Back Panel**: Full back panel for stability

## Hardware
- **Hinges**: Concealed, 6-way adjustable, soft-close
- **Drawer Slides**: Full-extension, soft-close ball-bearing slides
- **Overlay**: Full overlay (doors cover the face frame completely)
- **Handles/Knobs**: NOT included — customer selects separately

## What Sets Kitchen Crest Apart
1. **No Particle Board**: Zero particle board in any component — doors, frames, or panels
2. **Soft-Close Standard**: Every door and drawer includes soft-close hardware at no extra cost
3. **UV-Coated Interiors**: Natural wood interiors with UV protective coating for durability
4. **Concealed Hinges**: Clean exterior appearance — no visible hinge hardware
5. **Full Overlay**: Modern, seamless appearance with doors covering the full face frame

## Assembly Options
- **RTA (Ready-to-Assemble)**: Ships flat-packed with all hardware included. Assembly required. Ships in 2-3 business days. Most cost-effective option.
- **Pre-Assembled**: Arrives fully built, ready for installation. Ships in 3-5 business days. Higher cost but saves installation time.

## Maintenance & Care
- Clean with mild soap and water
- Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasives
- Touch-up kits available for each color ($60)
- Color-matched spray paint available ($150)

# Estate Cabinet Lines — SKU Reference

## Estate Line Overview
The Estate collection features a Recessed Panel door style with clean, traditional lines. Available in three colors: Black (EB), White (EW), and Sage Green (ES). All Estate lines share the same construction quality as the Shaker lines — solid wood door frames, fiberboard center panels, UV-coated interiors, concealed soft-close hinges, and full overlay design.

Estate lines carry a 15% premium over the Shaker White baseline.

## Estate Black (EB)
- **SKU Prefix**: EB
- **Style**: Recessed Panel
- **Color**: Black
- **Available**: RTA and pre-assembled

SKU naming follows same convention as Shaker White:
- EB-B12 through EB-B42 (Base Cabinets)
- EB-W series (Wall Cabinets)
- EB-3DB, EB-2DB (Drawer Bases)
- EB-SB, EB-BBC, EB-CSB (Specialty)
- EB-U, EB-O, EB-MO (Tall/Pantry/Oven)
- EB-VS, EB-VSD (Vanity)

## Estate White (EW)
- **SKU Prefix**: EW
- **Style**: Recessed Panel
- **Color**: White
- **Available**: RTA and pre-assembled

Same SKU structure as Estate Black with EW prefix.

## Estate Sage (ES)
- **SKU Prefix**: ES
- **Style**: Recessed Panel
- **Color**: Sage Green
- **Available**: RTA and pre-assembled

Same SKU structure as Estate Black with ES prefix.

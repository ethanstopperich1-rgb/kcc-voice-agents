# Kitchen Crest Cabinets — Policies

## Return Policy
- **Damage/Shortage Reporting**: Report within 72 hours of delivery with photos
- **Returns**: Accepted within 30 days in original packaging and resalable condition
- **Restocking Fee**: 25% on assembled/pre-assembled items
- **Non-Returnable Items**: Refrigerator panels, shelf boards, toe kicks, back panels
- **Condition**: Items must be in original packaging, uninstalled, and resalable
- **Process**: Contact your designer or the Orlando office to initiate a return

## Shipping & Delivery
- **Local Delivery**: Within 50 miles of any warehouse location via Kitchen Crest couriers
- **RTA Lead Time**: 2-3 business days from order confirmation
- **Pre-Assembled Lead Time**: 3-5 business days from order confirmation
- **Freight Shipping**: Available for deliveries beyond 50-mile radius
- **Delivery Notification**: Courier will call 30 minutes before arrival
- **Receiving**: Inspect all items at delivery; note any damage on delivery receipt

## Payment Terms
- Payment terms vary by customer account type
- Contact accounting team for specific payment terms
- Kitchen Crest does NOT process payments through AI phone system
- For payment questions, request transfer to accounting team

## Business Model
- Kitchen Crest is strictly B2B wholesale
- Serves: contractors, retailers, builders, kitchen designers, multi-family developers, commercial builders, OEMs, and distributors
- Homeowners should work through a contractor or designer
- Volume discounts available for qualifying accounts

## Quality Standards
- No particle board in any cabinet line
- Solid wood door frames on all cabinets
- UV-coated natural wood interiors
- Concealed soft-close hinges standard
- Full overlay door design
- Fiberboard center panels (not particle board)

## Sample Policy
- Sample doors: $33 per sample, 11" x 15"
- Recommended before placing full orders
- Available for all 7 cabinet lines
- Color-matched to production cabinets

## Warranty
- Contact Kitchen Crest team for current warranty details
- Warranty information provided at time of purchase

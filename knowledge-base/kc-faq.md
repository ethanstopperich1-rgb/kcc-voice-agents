# Kitchen Crest Cabinets — Frequently Asked Questions

## What cabinet lines do you carry?
We offer 7 lines across two style families. Shaker style: Shaker White (SW), Light Gray (LG), Shaker Navy (SN), and Modern Wood (MW). Estate style (Recessed Panel): Estate Black (EB), Estate White (EW), and Estate Sage (ES).

## What is your lead time?
RTA (Ready-to-Assemble) cabinets ship in 2-3 business days. Pre-assembled cabinets ship in 3-5 business days. Orders can sometimes ship sooner depending on inventory availability.

## Do you ship nationwide?
We deliver within 50 miles of our warehouse locations using our own local couriers. Our warehouses are in Melrose Park IL (HQ), San Antonio TX, Orlando FL, Miami FL, and Madison WI. For deliveries beyond 50 miles, freight shipping is available.

## Can I get a sample door?
Yes! Sample doors are $33 each and measure 11" x 15". We highly recommend ordering a sample before placing your full order to confirm the look and feel in your project space.

## Do you use particle board?
No. Kitchen Crest does NOT use particle board in any cabinet line. All doors use solid wood frames with fiberboard center panels. Interiors feature UV-coated natural wood.

## What hardware comes standard?
All cabinets include concealed hinges with soft-close mechanisms. Full overlay design is standard across all lines. No additional hardware purchase is needed for doors.

## What is your return policy?
Report any damage or shortages within 72 hours of delivery. Returns are accepted within 30 days in original packaging and resalable condition. A 25% restocking fee applies to assembled items. Non-returnable items include: refrigerator panels, shelf boards, toe kicks, and back panels.

## Do contractors get discounts?
Yes! Contractors, repeat customers, builders, and designers receive volume-based discounts. Contact us for volume pricing specific to your project size.

## Do you provide installation?
No, Kitchen Crest does not provide installation services. However, we can answer assembly questions and provide guidance. Pre-assembled cabinets are available for faster installation. Ask your designer for installer referrals in your area.

## What is the difference between RTA and pre-assembled?
RTA (Ready-to-Assemble) cabinets ship flat-packed and require assembly on-site. They cost less and ship faster (2-3 business days). Pre-assembled cabinets arrive fully built and ready to install — they ship in 3-5 business days and cost more due to labor.

## Do you sell to homeowners?
Kitchen Crest is a B2B wholesale distributor. We primarily serve contractors, builders, designers, multi-family developers, commercial builders, OEMs, and distributors. Homeowners should work with a contractor or designer who can order through us.

## What is the warranty?
Please contact our team for current warranty information specific to your project and order.

## Can I customize cabinet sizes?
Kitchen Crest offers standard cabinet sizes. For non-standard requirements, speak with one of our designers who can help find the best solution using our standard SKU range, including fillers and specialty pieces.

## What payment methods do you accept?
Please contact our accounting team for payment terms and accepted methods. We do not process payments through our AI phone system.

# Kitchen Crest Cabinets — Product Catalog

## Company Overview
Kitchen Crest Cabinets is a B2B wholesale RTA (Ready-to-Assemble) cabinet distributor founded in July 2020. We serve contractors, builders, designers, multi-family developers, commercial builders, OEMs, and distributors. Kitchen Crest does NOT sell direct to homeowners.

## Construction & Materials
- **Doors**: Solid wood frames — NO particle board in any line
- **Center Panels**: Fiberboard (not particle board)
- **Interior**: UV-coated natural wood
- **Hinges**: Concealed, soft-close
- **Overlay**: Full overlay
- **Hardware**: Soft-close standard on all lines
- **Assembly**: Available as RTA (flat-box) and pre-assembled

## Lead Times
- **RTA (Ready-to-Assemble)**: Ships in 2-3 business days
- **Pre-Assembled**: Ships in 3-5 business days
- Orders can sometimes ship sooner depending on inventory

## Cabinet Categories

### Base Cabinets
Standard height 34.5", depth 24". Available in single-door (12"-27") and double-door (33"-42") configurations. All base cabinets include 1 adjustable shelf and drawer(s).

### Wall/Upper Cabinets
Standard depth 12". Heights range from 12" to 42". Available in standard and glass door (GD) variants. Widths: 9", 12", 15", 18", 21", 24", 27", 30", 33", 36", 39", 42".

### Drawer Base Cabinets
Full drawer stack cabinets — 3-drawer (3DB) and 2-door-with-drawer (2DB) configurations.
- 3DB available in 18", 27", 36" widths
- 2DB available in 30", 36" widths

### Specialty Cabinets
- **Sink Bases (SB)**: 27" and 36" widths
- **Blind Base Corners (BBC)**: 36", 39", 45" widths
- **Corner Sink Base (CSB)**: 36" width
- **Lazy Susan**: Corner cabinet with rotating shelves

### Tall & Pantry Cabinets
- **Wall Pantry (U-series)**: 30" wide, heights 84", 90", 96", depth 24"
- **Oven Cabinet (O-series)**: 30" wide, heights 84" and 96"
- **Microwave Cabinet (MO)**: 30" wide, heights 18" and 36"

### Vanity Cabinets
- **Vanity Sink Base (VS)**: 36" wide, height 34.5", depth 21"
- **Vanity Sink with Drawers (VSD)**: 48" and 72" widths
